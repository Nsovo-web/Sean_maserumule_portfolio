About Me

I'm a BSc Computing Graduate with deep roots in full-stack web development, software engineering, and data science. I care about building things that actually work for people — not just on a demo screen.

One of my most meaningful projects was a farm management system I built for my father's poultry business. It replaced stacks of paper records with a desktop app that tracks flock health, egg sales, employee input, and profitability — season by season.

That experience shaped how I approach every project: understand the real problem first, then engineer the right solution. Whether it's a web app, a data pipeline, or a desktop tool — I build with purpose.
